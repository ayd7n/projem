Komut satiri uzerinden siteye ve veritabanina erisim talimatlari:

1. PowerShell veya CMD acin.

2. Siteyi test etmek icin:
   curl.exe http://localhost/projem/

3. Login islemi icin:
   curl.exe -X POST -d "username=admin@parfum.com&password=12345" http://localhost/projem/login.php

4. Korumali sayfaya oturum cerezini tasiyarak gitmek icin:
   curl.exe -c cookie_cli.txt -b cookie_cli.txt -L -X POST -d "username=admin@parfum.com&password=12345" http://localhost/projem/login.php
   curl.exe -b cookie_cli.txt http://localhost/projem/navigation.php

Not:
- PowerShell'de `curl` yerine `curl.exe` kullanin. `curl` takma adi `Invoke-WebRequest` oldugu icin `-X` ve `-d` parametrelerinde hata verir.

Veritabanina erismek icin:
C:\xampp\mysql\bin\mysql.exe -h localhost -u root parfum_erp

Server hatalari icin loglar:
C:\xampp\apache\logs
